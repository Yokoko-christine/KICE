Welcome to KICE

We are an NGO in cooperated and Limited by liability with a slogan to give priority to Humanity. we have thematic areas in Environmental Governance to protect and conserve nature, child protection, Women protection and empowerment, General protection, Water Sanitation and Hygiene(WASH) Mental Health, General health, Financial inclusion, Education and Agricultural innovations.

About Us

Kenal Initiative for Community Empowerment (KICE) was founded with the goal of transforming communities through locally driven solutions. We believe that sustainable development is achieved through inclusive participation, innovation, and shared responsibility.

Our Products

Our key community-focused products include:

  • Educational materials for rural schools
  • Solar-powered household kits
