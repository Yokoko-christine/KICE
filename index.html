Welcome to KICE

We are an NGO in cooperated and Limited by liability with a slogan to give priority to Humanity. we have thematic areas in Environmental Governance to protect and conserve nature, child protection, Women protection and empowerment, General protection, Water Sanitation and Hygiene(WASH) Mental Health, General health, Financial inclusion, Education and Agricultural innovations.

About Us

Kenal Initiative for Community Empowerment (KICE) was founded with the goal of transforming communities through locally driven solutions. We believe that sustainable development is achieved through inclusive participation, innovation, and shared responsibility.

Our Products

KICE supports local farmers by availing sustainable and innovative agricultural products tailored to boost productivity, build resilience to climate change, and promote food security.

HYBRID DROUGHT-RESISTANT SEEDS

  • Drought-resistant Maize Seeds Early-maturing, high-yield maize that withstands water stress, ideal for semi-arid regions.
  • Improved Sorghum and Millet Seeds Heat-tolerant grains rich in nutrition and highly adaptable to dry land farming.
  • Hybrid Bean Seeds (NABE varieties) Fast-growing, disease-resistant beans with excellent yields, even in nutrient-depleted soils.
  • Climate-smart Vegetable Seeds (e.g., Amaranth, Eggplant, Okra) Suitable for smallholder kitchen gardens, improving household nutrition and income.

AGRICULTURAL TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT

  • Hand Hoes and Rippers Durable tools for land preparation that reduce strain and improve productivity.
  • Seed Planters (Manual & Semi-automatic) Tools that enable uniform planting with less seed wastage and better spacing.
  • Knapsack Sprayers Lightweight sprayers used for applying pesticides, fertilizers, or herbicides with ease and precision.
  • Watering Cans and Drip Kits Ideal for vegetable and fruit farmers practicing irrigation, especially in dry areas.
  • Post-harvest Tools (e.g., Drying tarpaulins, Shellers) Help reduce post-harvest losses and improve quality and market value of produce.

Our Services & Thematic Areas

  • ENVIRONMENTAL GOVERNANCE

    • Protecting and conserving natural resources We lead community-driven conservation efforts to safeguard forests, water bodies, and biodiversity, promoting eco-friendly practices and policies that reduce environmental degradation.

  • PROTECTION

    • Child Protection We implement programs that shield children from abuse, neglect, exploitation, and harmful practices, while supporting child rights and well-being.
    Women Protection and Empowerment (WPE) We empower women through rights awareness, psychosocial support, and economic initiatives to combat gender-based violence and enhance agency.
    General Protection Our interventions ensure access to justice, safety, and basic rights for marginalized groups, including persons with disabilities, elderly, and displaced persons.

  • FINANCIAL INCLUSION

    • VSLA Financial Inclusion Scheme We support Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA) that enable communities—especially women and youth—to save, access affordable credit, and invest in small businesses for self-reliance.

  • PUBLIC HEALTH

    • Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH ) We promote safe water access, good hygiene practices, and proper sanitation to prevent disease outbreaks and improve quality of life.
    Mental Health We run community-based mental health awareness and support systems to tackle stigma, provide counseling, and improve emotional well-being.

  • AGRICULTURAL INNOVATIONS

    • Introduction of Hybrid Seeds We provide access to drought-resistant, high-yield hybrid seeds to support farmers in adapting to changing weather patterns.
    Climate Smart Agriculture We promote sustainable farming practices like mulching, water harvesting, and organic composting to mitigate climate change impacts.
    Improved Agricultural Technology We introduce modern tools, mechanization, and training to boost productivity, reduce labor, and increase income from farming.

  • EDUCATION

    • Community Hands-On Skilling: We provide vocational training and entrepreneurship skills to youth and women, fostering financial independence and resilience.
    Capacity Building for Schools: We support primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions with administrative training, learning materials, and staff development to improve academic performance.
    Mentoring & Modeling Higher Learning Institutions We guide institutions in designing learner-centered programs, mentorship models, and technical training that prepare students for real-world challenges.
